3D Cube Visualization

Published: May 2026

A simple interactive cube rendered in real time with Three.js. Drag the canvas to rotate the cube and observe how the MeshNormal shader colors each face based on the surface normal direction—an elegant way to make 3D geometry legible at a glance.

About This Visualization

  • Interactive 3D Cube
  • MeshNormal shading

Drag the canvas to rotate the cube. The renderer adapts to the viewport size and runs entirely in the browser using WebGL.

Interaction: Drag to rotate. Resize the window to adjust the canvas.

Although the cube is the simplest of the Platonic solids, it is also one of the most useful test subjects in computer graphics. It exposes lighting, shading, projection, and interaction in a form that is easy to read but rich enough to reveal the behavior of the underlying renderer.

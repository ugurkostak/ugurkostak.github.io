The Ulam Spiral is a simple but striking pattern discovered by mathematician Stanislaw Ulam. When natural numbers are arranged in a spiral and primes are highlighted, surprising diagonal and linear patterns emerge—suggesting deep mathematical structure underlying the distribution of primes.

This visualization displays the first 10,000 numbers arranged in a spiral pattern, with prime numbers highlighted in red.
